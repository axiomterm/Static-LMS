## Welcome!

This portal brings together the organization's **internal courses**. Each course is a
simple page with the material you need: text, and when applicable, videos and
downloadable documents.

### How to navigate

1. Go back to the catalog from the **"All courses"** link in the header.
2. Open the course you're interested in.
3. If the course has a video, it plays directly on the page.
4. Attached documents appear at the end for download.

> You don't need a username or password: access is validated automatically with your
> domain account.

Questions? Write to **training@your-organization.local**.
